FAQ

Why subscriptions and not hiring full-time?

Mainly to have convenient and flexible access to design work. You can pause or cancel the subscription anytime so you don't have to pay for a full time designer during periods of downtime when you don't have enough work to keep them busy.

How fast will I receive my designs?

On average most requests get delivered in 2 days or less. However for more complex requests it can take longer and you will receive updates every 1-2 days to stay informed on the progress.

How do I get started with your design services?

It's easy to get started! You can choose to either subscribe to the monthly design and Webflow development service or if you want a custom quote for a one-time project get in touch with me via silvestrs.b@gmail.com and we will price the project seperately.

Can I cancel or pause my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel or pause your subscription at any time. If you pause your subscription your remaining days can be used anytime in the future.
